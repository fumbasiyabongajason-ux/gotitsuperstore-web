Sephora doesn't ship to SA — here's what to do

Hunting for Sephora in South Africa? There's no local store and no direct shipping. The good news: several SA beauty retailers stock many of the same global brands — here's where to look.

Let's clear it up first: Sephora does not have a South African store and does not ship to South Africa directly. Searches for "Sephora South Africa" usually end in disappointment. But you don't actually need Sephora — most of the global brands people want from it are available through South African beauty retailers, with local delivery and no customs surprises.

Best South African beauty retailers to try instead

These local stores carry a wide range of premium and everyday beauty, and many stock the same international brands you'd browse at Sephora:

  • Clicks — huge beauty range, frequent promotions, stores nationwide plus online delivery.
  • Dis-Chem — broad cosmetics and skincare selection, including premium counters, with online ordering.
  • Woolworths Beauty — curated skincare and makeup with reliable local delivery.
  • Foschini / The Foschini Group beauty — fashion-retail beauty halls stocking many global names.
  • Truworths and selected department stores — growing beauty ranges.
  • Online beauty specialists (e.g. Superbalist and Takealot beauty) — convenient delivery and a wide brand spread.
Tip: brand availability shifts over time, so if you're after one specific product, search a couple of these retailers directly. You'll often find it sitting in stock locally — no forwarding, no duty.

Really want it from Sephora? Use a forwarder

If a particular Sephora-exclusive is non-negotiable, you can use a freight forwarder (such as Aramex Global Shopper or MyUS): you get a US address, Sephora ships there, and the forwarder sends it on. Expect the forwarding fee plus customs duty and 15% VAT since the 2024 SARS change1 — and note that some liquids and aerosols have shipping restrictions. For most shoppers, a local retailer is simpler, cheaper and faster.

Frequently asked questions

Does Sephora ship to South Africa?

No. Sephora has no South African store and does not ship to South Africa directly. To get Sephora products you'd need a freight forwarder, or you can buy comparable brands from local beauty retailers.

What is the best Sephora alternative in South Africa?

Clicks and Dis-Chem are the biggest, with wide premium and everyday beauty ranges and local delivery. Woolworths Beauty, Foschini's beauty halls, and online specialists like Superbalist and Takealot beauty are strong options too.

Can I get Sephora-exclusive brands in South Africa?

Sometimes. Many global brands sold at Sephora are stocked by South African retailers. For a true Sephora-exclusive, a freight forwarder is the only route, but expect forwarding fees plus customs duty and 15% VAT.

Is it worth forwarding Sephora orders to SA?

Usually only for a specific item you can't find locally. Between forwarding fees, duty and VAT, and shipping restrictions on some liquids, a local beauty retailer is generally cheaper and faster.
