Mr D vs Uber Eats in South Africa

Two apps dominate food delivery in South Africa — locally-grown Mr D and global Uber Eats. They work differently and shine in different places. Here's how to choose.

When dinner needs to arrive at your door, most South Africans reach for one of two apps: Mr D (the home-grown service owned by Takealot) or Uber Eats (the global player). Both are solid; the right pick depends on where you live and what you're ordering.

How they differ

⚠️ Fees move around. Both apps price delivery dynamically — by distance, restaurant and demand — and sometimes add service fees, so the cheapest app varies order to order. Always check the fee in-app before you check out.

Which should you open tonight?

• Outside the big metros? Mr D often has the wider reach, thanks to its national footprint.
• In a major city wanting maximum choice? Uber Eats usually lists a deep set of restaurants with slick live tracking.
• Want one app for food and groceries/retail? Mr D's link to Takealot and grocery partners is handy.
• Chasing the best price? Compare both for your specific address and order — the winner changes.

Bottom line: Uber Eats tends to win on big-city restaurant depth; Mr D tends to win on national coverage and its local retail ecosystem. Keep both installed and let each order decide.

Frequently asked questions

Is Mr D or Uber Eats better in South Africa?

It depends on location. Mr D (owned by Takealot) has a wide national footprint, including smaller towns, and also delivers groceries and retail. Uber Eats is strongest in major metros with deep restaurant choice and live tracking. Many users keep both.

Does Mr D deliver more than food?

Yes. Beyond restaurants, Mr D delivers groceries and other retail and is tied into the Takealot ecosystem, including a grocery partnership, so it can cover more than a typical food-only app.

Which is cheaper, Mr D or Uber Eats?

There's no fixed winner. Both price delivery dynamically by distance, restaurant and demand, and may add service fees, so the cheaper option changes per order. Check the fee in each app before ordering.

Is Uber Eats available across South Africa?

Uber Eats is strongest in the major metros such as Cape Town, Johannesburg and Durban and continues to expand. For smaller towns, Mr D's national coverage is often wider.
