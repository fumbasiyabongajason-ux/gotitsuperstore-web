Checkers Sixty60 vs Woolworths Dash

South Africa's two best-known on-demand grocery apps both promise groceries at your door within the hour — but in 2026 their delivery fees and strengths have diverged. Here's the honest comparison.

On-demand grocery delivery is now a daily habit for millions of South Africans, and two names dominate the conversation: Checkers Sixty60 and Woolworths Dash. Both deliver groceries in about an hour. The big 2026 story is price: after nearly six years of a flat R35 fee across the market, the fees have started to move.

What the fee change really means

For almost six years, every major player — Sixty60, Dash, Pick n Pay asap! and Spar2U — held the line at R35. That ended when Woolworths raised Dash to R45 on 10 November 2025, a 29% jump, citing rising costs.12 Checkers had already nudged Sixty60 to R36, and a further rise to R37 lands on 1 July 2026.3 So in 2026 Sixty60 is the cheaper delivery, while Dash leans on Woolworths' premium food reputation.

Which should you use?

• Pick Sixty60 for the lowest delivery fee, the widest range (including Checkers Hyper big-ticket items), and a subscription that wipes out delivery costs if you order often.
• Pick Dash if you specifically want Woolworths' food quality and own-brand range and don't mind paying more per drop.
• Order often? Sixty60's Xtra Savings Plus can pay for itself quickly on delivery fees alone.

Bottom line: on price and range, Sixty60 leads in 2026; on premium groceries, Dash holds its niche. Many households simply keep both and choose per shop.

Frequently asked questions

Is Sixty60 or Woolworths Dash cheaper?

Sixty60 is cheaper on delivery in 2026: it charges R36 (rising to R37 on 1 July 2026), while Woolworths Dash rose to R45 on 10 November 2025. Sixty60 also offers an Xtra Savings Plus subscription with unlimited free delivery.

How much is Woolworths Dash delivery?

Woolworths Dash charges R45 per delivery, effective 10 November 2025 — a 29% increase from the long-standing R35 fee, which Woolworths attributed to rising costs.

How fast is Checkers Sixty60?

Sixty60 promises grocery delivery within 60 minutes in its service areas, and has offered a service guarantee crediting the delivery fee if it misses the promise. It operates from Checkers, Shoprite and Checkers Hyper.

Which has a bigger range, Sixty60 or Dash?

Sixty60 has the broader range, adding bigger-ticket items from Checkers Hyper (like electronics and camping gear) alongside groceries. Dash focuses on Woolworths' premium food and everyday essentials.
