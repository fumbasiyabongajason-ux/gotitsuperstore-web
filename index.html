About us

A curated affiliate marketplace

Got It Superstore is a curated affiliate marketplace that helps South African and global shoppers discover the best deals from trusted retailers worldwide. We do not sell products directly — we surface verified, hand-picked items from ASOS, Rakuten, Zalando, Sephora, Wayfair, Shein, Apple, Etsy, eBay, AliExpress, Takealot, IKEA, and Costco, so you find quality products at competitive prices without the hours of comparison shopping.

When you click through our links and make a purchase, we earn a small commission from the retailer at no extra cost to you. That commission funds the curation, content, and editorial review work — and lets us keep the entire site free for shoppers.

We're registered in South Africa as a private company under CIPC Reg. 2023/224652/07, and we comply with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and the US FTC Endorsement Guidelines.
