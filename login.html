What we keep · what we don't.

Your sign-in unlocks the mall door for daily entry. That's it.

  • Got It Superstore never has access to your personal information. Passwords are not transmitted to us; Google sign-ins happen entirely between you and Google.
  • We keep an anonymous session token on this device so the mall door opens for you next time. No name, no email, no profile data is read by the site.
  • We count anonymous visits (sessions only) so the owner can see how busy the mall is — never who visited.
  • No cookies, no third-party trackers. Just a single key in your browser's local storage. Clearing it signs you out.
